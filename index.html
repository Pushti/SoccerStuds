FEATURES

Increase revenue

Raising money for your club has never been so easy. We've dedicated areas for sponsors, an online cashback scheme and an integrated e-commerce platform.

Easy to build website

Customise your website's layout with different colours, headers and backgrounds. Get your website looking just the way you want.

Admin saving features

Your free club website comes with every tool required to create a spanking new home for your club, allowing you to manage all your activities online.

Improve communication

Email and text alerts, maps and directions - it's all been thought of and is only just a click away. Plus we're always adding new stuff to make your life even easier.

Mobile access

Get the same great online experience when you're out and about. View your website via a mobile device. Our service has been fully optimised.

Be part of something big

An online community dedicated to grassroots football. Get access to expert training tips, monthly e-newsletters, competitions and much more.

Customise your website

Make your website stand out from the crowd by choosing your own colour scheme and background. Give it that personal touch by adding your own club logo or website header.

Turn sections of the site on and off at the click of a button to suit your needs, making your website as simple or as detailed as you want.

Drag and drop website builder

Drag and drop sections of your website to get your homepage looking exactly as you want it.

Want to tell the world you're top of the league? Move the league table to the top of the page!

Want to raise more club funds by giving your main sponsor greater prominence? Easily done!

Team and fixtures management

Our club websites have everything you need to manage your team online: fixtures and results, league tables, player profiles, match day line-ups, detailed squad stats and much more.

Whatever info your team want to record and post on your website, you can have it online in no time at all!

Squad statistics and match reports

Your club website will allow you to record individual statistics for each and every game.

Choose the starting line up and subs for each of your matches via easy to use drop down menus. Record appearances, goals, penalties, assists, yellow/red cards and the star player for each fixture.

The website will automatically keep a running total of all of these statistics as the season unfolds. This allows your players to track their performances and statistics throughout the season.

Your players can view their statistics at any time on the main website by viewing the squad statistics page.

In addition, your website can capture individual match reports for each of your fixtures.

Multimedia features

You've captured all those magic moments on the field of play – now relive them all on your club website!

Upload and share photos and videos straight to your website for all of your club members to enjoy time and time again.

Fundraising

We know that fundraising can be the toughest job at a football club, which is why we provide a number of ways for you to raise extra funds for your club via your website.

Earn more money for your club by allowing sponsors to advertise on your website's dedicated sponsors' area – great for local businesses!

We've teamed up with the Football Foundation to help you earn cash for your club every time you shop online, whilst our e-commerce platform lets you sell club merchandise via your club website.

Please read 'Football Foundation' and 'E-Commerce' to find out more.

Football Foundation

We've teamed up with the Football Foundation - the UK's largest sports charity - to help your football club raise much-needed funds every time any club member shops online.

Simply visit the Club Cashback section of your website, browse over 2,000 major high-street retailers and, for every single item purchased, your club earns a cashback reward of between 1-15% of the purchase value.

It's money for nothing, with every single penny of the reward going back to your club.

The Football Foundation and Club Website – supporting grassroots football.

E-commerce

Your club website will come with its very own Club Shop, allowing you to sell merchandise directly from your website with ease.

Whether it's the latest replica kit, training gear or memorabilia, club members and supporters alike can select from your range of products and purchase securely using our integrated PayPal system.

Raising money for your club has never been so easy!

SMS text and email

Ease the burden of club communication with our integrated email and SMS platform.

Remove the need for countless emails and endless texts – just write one message and send it to all of your players, coaches and parents at the click of a mouse!

Mobile-optimised

We know how precious your time is. You're always on the go, so we've made a mobile-optimised version of your club website for use on your smartphone.

Now anyone can view your website while they're on the move. You no longer need to be in front of your computer to view match reports, league tables and latest news announcements – all these are now accessible direct from your phone while you're out and about.

Simple to use wherever you are - Club Website keeps you on the move.

Full customer support

On those rare occasions when you need help with your website, just get in touch with our Customer Support Team.

We pride ourselves on our response time and our success rate of resolving your problems - and our members agree!

Just get in touch and give us a try - we're confident you'll find our customer support is second to none.

Football refers to a number of sports that involve, to varying degrees, kicking a ball with the foot to score a goal. The most popular of these sports worldwide is association football, more commonly known as just "football" or "soccer". Unqualified, the word football applies to whichever form of football is the most popular in the regional context in which the word appears, including association football, as well as American football, Australian rules football, Canadian football, Gaelic football, rugby league, rugby union,[1] and other related games. These variations of football are known as football codes.
